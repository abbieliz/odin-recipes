Ham And Bananas Hollandaise

Just like mom used to make!

A brilliant way to jazz up ordinary, boring bananas.

Perfect for those busy weeknights when you just need something fast and delicious to please the whole family!

Ingredients

  • 6 medium bananas
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 6 thin slices of boiled ham
  • 3 tablespoons prepared mustard
  • 2 envelopes (1 1/4-oz size) hollandaise sauce mix
  • 1/4 cup light cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F. Lightly butter 2-quart, shallow baking dish
  2. Peel bananas; sprinkle each with 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice, to prevent darkening
  3. Spread ham slices with mustard. Wrap each banana in slice of ham. Arrange in single layer in casserole. Bake 10 minutes
  4. For the sauce, in a small saucepan, combine sauce mix with 1 cup water, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, and cream
  5. Heat, stirring, to boiling; pour over bananas
  6. Bake 5 minutes longer, or until slightly golden

Nice with a fresh green salad for brunch or lunch!
