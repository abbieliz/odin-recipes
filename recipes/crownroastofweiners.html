Crown Roast of Frankfurters

A classic crowd pleaser!

This crown roast of weiners is the epitome of simple and sophisticated.

Whip out this decadent dish on any special occasion!

Ingredients

  • 20 frankfurters
  • 2 cups of sauerkraut, cooked

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Arrange frankfurters side by side
  3. Using a large needle and string, sew through frankfurters 1/2 inch from the bottoms
  4. Sew through frankfurters 1/2 inch from the tops
  5. Tie strings together at the top making a half circle
  6. Repeat with bottom string to create another half circle
  7. Stand circle of frankfurters on end to form "crown"
  8. Fill center of crown with sauerkraut
  9. Bake filled crown in moderate oven, about 20 minutes

You can fill crown with stuffing, creamed cabbage, creamed cauliflower or potato balls instead of sauerkraut.
